Cosmorita Cocktail

Dive into the world of the divine with a classy cocktail that inspires you to dream about the luxuries of life. A drink that adds the spark to margaritas and entices the desires of your heart.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of silver tequila
  • 1 shot of triple sec
  • 1/2 shot of lemon juice
  • 1/2 shot of lime juice
  • 1/2 of cranberry juice
  • 1 cup of ice
  • 1 lime sliced

Steps

  1. Place all ice cubes in a Shaker
  2. Add a pinch of salt to the Shaker (optional)
  3. Add 2 slices of lime in the Shaker
  4. Add all remaining ingredients to the Shaker
  5. Thoroughly shake until frosty
  6. Empty the blended drink in the glass and garnish with a lime slice
