Long Island Iced Tea

Breeze The Worries Away

A go to drink for a stressful week. Nothing brings the weekend vibes like a Long Island Iced Tea. Trying to leave your burdens behind and unwind? Then sip away!

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of gin
  • 1 shot of premium rum
  • 1 shot of vodka
  • 1 shot of sweet and sour mix
  • 1 shot of triple sec
  • 1/2 cup of classic Coca-Cola
  • 1 cup of ice
  • 1 lime slice

Steps

  1. Place all ice cubes in a glass
  2. Add all alcohol drinks
  3. Slightly stir with a straw
  4. Add Coca-Cola and remianing liquids
  5. Gently stir drink with a straw
  6. Place lime slice on rim and enjoy
